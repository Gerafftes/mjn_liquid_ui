# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in `mjn_liquid_ui` to be respectful, constructive, and
welcoming regardless of experience, identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific to the code, documentation, or behavior being
  discussed.
- Accept correction and resolve disagreements with technical evidence.
- Protect private information and respect responsible security disclosure.
- Help keep issues and pull requests focused and useful.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or repeated off-topic comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This code applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews,
and other project spaces. It also applies when someone represents the project
in public.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact options on the
[@Gerafftes GitHub profile](https://github.com/Gerafftes). Do not publish
sensitive reports in a public issue. Include relevant links, context, and any
supporting material so the report can be reviewed fairly.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit or remove contributions that violate this policy and
may issue a warning, temporary restriction, or permanent ban depending on the
severity and history of the behavior. Reports will be handled as privately as
reasonably possible, and retaliation is not permitted.

This policy is based on the principles of the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
